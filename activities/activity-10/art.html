Wooden carvings are a traditional art form that has been passed down through generations. They often feature detailed designs and are made from various types of wood.

Artists use different tools and techniques to create unique pieces, ranging from small figurines to large sculptures. The art of woodcarving requires patience, skill, and creativity.

Many cultures around the world have their own styles of woodcarving, reflecting their history and traditions.
