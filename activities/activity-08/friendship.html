The Friendship

The Friendship is a full scale replica of an eighteenth century merchant ship that represents Salem’s maritime trade history.

This replica ship is a popular attraction in Salem and offers visitors a chance to learn about the maritime history of the region.

The Friendship of Salem is docked along the waterfront, surrounded by historic homes that reflect Salem’s rich maritime heritage and architectural history.

Visitors can explore the ship and the surrounding area to gain a deeper understanding of Salem’s maritime past and its influence on the city’s development.
